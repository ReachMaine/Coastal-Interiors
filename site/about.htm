Lori Chase, CKD, is the owner of Coastal Interiors. She originally was a graphic artist for newsprint, and then ran with scissors until she became a textile artist for 15 years. Being a part of the home construction industry for over 25 years has led her interior design. She obtained her NKBA Certified Kitchen Designer certification 5 years ago, to enable her to assist clients with highest skills possible. With extensive training and experience in all the products offered by Coastal Interiors she has the design and technical background required to complete any size project.
“As the ultimate mixed media collage, home interior design has so many possibilities and challenges, it is extremely rewarding to view a completed project and fulfill the client’s dreams. Helping to inspire and inform the clients about choices available and then detailing the steps required to obtain their goal is the key.”

Coastal Interiors has 3 fulltime designers on staff to help you. We emphasize team approach to your projects, both in house with our other designers and in the field with the contractors.

We also co-ordinate with professional contractors to help ensure the completed installation is of a quality that endure.
