Coastal Interiors is a design showroom for interiors finishes. We can help you with every interior space need. From budget planning to the finished outcome, Coastal Interiors is committed to creating the space you want to live in. No matter the project size or budget, we can help you.

We carry flooring, wall coverings, cabinetry and counters, plumbing, lighting, hardware and window treatments and upholstery needs.

Whether the style is classic, contemporary, cottage or ‘green’, we carry the products and design/product knowledge to help you complete your new home or renovation project with the information you need. We can help as little or as much as you need.

Located on the banks of the Union River in downtown Ellsworth, we have 7000 square feet of display showroom. All the classic styles and the newest products available are on display. We install most of what we sell and work closely with the builders to help ensure the end result is what you wanted. Through out the process we assist, inform and help you create the unique personalized space you wanted. We inform, inspire, and assist our clients through the process of transforming their home into a personalized expression of themselves and add to their enjoyment and productivity of their interior space.

Professional designers in the area, contractors, homeowners and our clients, use our extensive design showroom.
